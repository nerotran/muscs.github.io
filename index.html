{% markdown %} Hi! I am Suman Bhunia, a PhD student in the [Department of Computer Science at University of Nevada, Reno (UNR)][1]. I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant in the Computer Networking laboratory (CNL). My PhD supervisor is Prof. [Shamik Sengupta][2]. I am doing research on Security and vulnerability of Cognitive Radio Network. Before joining UNR, I was a PhD candidate at[Graduate Center of City University of New York (CUNY) ][3]. At CUNY, I was an adjunct lecturer for 2011-'12 academic year and Research Assistant for 2012-'13 academic year. I have finished my Bachelor of Technology degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from [Meghnad Saha Institute of technology][4], a college under [West Bengal university of technology][5]and Master of Technology in [Distributed and Mobile Computing][6] at The [Jadavpur University][7]. I did my My Master's thesis in the area of Reliable Data Transfer over WiMax network under the supervision of Prof. [Iti Saha Misra][8]. After the completion of M.Tech, I continued to work in the department of [Electronics and Telecommunication engineering][9] at Jadavpur University as Senior Research Fellow on a [Department of Science and Technology][10] funded project on Cognitive Radio Network.
